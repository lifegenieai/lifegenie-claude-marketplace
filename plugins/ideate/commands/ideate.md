# Ideate

Generate diverse ideas and possibilities around the user's topic or challenge.

## Behavior

1. Take the user's topic, problem, or prompt
2. Generate a range of ideas from practical to unconventional
3. Don't self-censor - include wild ideas alongside safe ones
4. Briefly note the potential of each idea
